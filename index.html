About Us

Welcome to our family-run Italian restaurant, where passion for authentic cuisine meets the warmth of home. Nestled in the heart of Boroughingham, we specialize in crafting delicious pizzas that capture the essence of Italy.

At our restaurant, every dish is made with love and the finest ingredients, sourced locally to ensure freshness and quality. Our menu is inspired by traditional recipes passed down through generations, offering a delightful array of flavors that represent the diverse regions of Italy. From our hand-tossed pizzas baked to perfection in a wood-fired oven to our delectable desserts, each meal is a celebration of Italian culinary heritage.

We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy good food and great company. Whether you're here for a casual lunch, a cozy dinner, or a special celebration, our dedicated team is committed to providing exceptional service to make your experience memorable.

Join us for a taste of Italy right in Boroughingham, and let us share our love for food with you. Buon appetito!

What makes our pizzas so special?

The Art of Hand-Tossed Pizza

Hand-tossed pizza is a culinary tradition that combines skill, passion, and artistry. This method involves stretching and tossing the dough by hand, creating a unique texture and shape that sets it apart from other styles. The process not only allows for a personalized touch but also ensures that each pizza is crafted with care, resulting in a crust that is both chewy and crispy.

The Magic of Wood-Fired Ovens

Baking pizza in a wood-fired oven elevates the experience to new heights. Our ovens reach extremely high temperatures, often exceeding 430°C, which allows the pizza to cook quickly and evenly. The intense heat creates a beautifully charred crust while locking in the flavors of the toppings. The wood adds a distinct smokiness that enhances the overall taste, making each bite a delightful experience. The combination of high heat and natural wood flavor creates a pizza that is truly exceptional.

Fresh Ingredients for Unmatched Flavor

At the heart of every great hand-tossed pizza is the quality of its ingredients. Using fresh, locally sourced produce, artisanal cheeses, and high-quality meats elevates the flavor profile of each pizza. Whether it’s vibrant tomatoes, fragrant basil, or creamy mozzarella, each ingredient plays a crucial role in creating a harmonious balance of flavors. Our commitment to freshness not only enhances the taste but also supports our local farmers and producers, making each pizza a celebration of this community, as well as sustainability.

A Culinary Experience to Savor

Enjoying one of our hand-tossed pizzas baked in a wood-fired oven is more than just a meal; it’s an experience to savor. The combination of the skilled craftsmanship involved in hand-tossing, the unique flavor imparted by the wood fire, and the use of fresh ingredients creates a pizza that is both delicious and memorable. Whether shared with friends and family or enjoyed solo, each bite transports you to a world of flavor and tradition, making it a perfect choice for any occasion.
